3D4E UCLA Campus Print Showcase

Utilized Blender and a LiDAR import plugin to generate a topographic model of the University of Utah campus. Collaborated with a campus department to obtain official building models, which were accurately scaled and positioned within the terrain environment. Produced a large-scale physical print using a Modix 120X industrial 3D printer. Represented the 3D Printing for Everyone (3D4E) Club at a regional showcase hosted at UCLA, presenting the project alongside partner 3D4E chapters from UCLA and USC.

Tools: Blender, Slicing software, FDM 3D printing

Midnight Hosting
